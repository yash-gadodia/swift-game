# Product Vision

## Vision Statement

Create a deeply social, low-friction pixel-art world where two players can meaningfully share progress in real time, starting from movement reliability and evolving toward a living co-op experience.

## Product North Star

"Two friends can jump in quickly, feel connected immediately, and keep coming back because shared moments are rewarding."

## Strategic Thesis

- Multiplayer trust is the core differentiator.
- Visual charm amplifies retention only if interaction is reliable.
- Technical credibility compounds into design freedom.

## Target Audience

Primary:
- Players who enjoy cozy simulation + light adventure loops
- Small friend groups seeking real-time co-op without complexity

Secondary:
- Mobile-native players seeking session-friendly co-op progression

## Product Pillars

1. Presence
- Players feel each other in-world with low latency and clear feedback.

2. Simplicity
- Joining sessions is intuitive and resilient.

3. Progression
- Shared goals evolve from movement -> tasks -> economy -> world growth.

4. Craft
- Pixel-art aesthetic with intentional, responsive controls.

## Experience Principles

- Immediate payoff: first successful co-op interaction in under 2 minutes.
- Readable state: players always know connection/session status.
- Graceful failure: disconnects never strand users.
- Consistent feel: control and movement behavior should be predictable.

## What We Are Not Building

- Massive open-world MMO infrastructure in early phases.
- Content-heavy single-player disguised as multiplayer.
- Server-cost-heavy systems before retention signal.

## Success Metrics (High-Level)

Technical:
- Session connect success rate
- Median and P95 input-to-remote-render latency
- Crash-free session rate

Product:
- % users completing first co-op session
- D1/D7 return among users who completed co-op session
- Session length and repeat co-op frequency

## Sequencing Philosophy

- Phase 1: Trust (network + sync)
- Phase 2: Loop (simple shared tasks)
- Phase 3: Depth (economy/progression)
- Phase 4: Polish (content scale + retention systems)
